ABOUT

Schweb Partners is a fully integrated Real Estate Acquisition and Management Firm founded by Abraham and Jacques. The two partners got their start in early 2005 buying single-family homes locally in their community. In the wake of the Great Financial Crisis in 2009, they turned their attention to multi-family, using their experience and knowledge earned from years of managing smaller properties to expanding to a multi-state portfolio of Apartment Communities. With the solid financial backing of a select group of close family offices, the past ten years have seen their portfolio grow to 5,000 plus units. Schweb Partners has expanded from the two founders into a strong team with a reputation for identifying opportunities, executing transactions quickly, and deploying an operations team that has proven their ability to execute on the founders vision and successfully add value to the property. In 2022 the firm began to expand into new asset classes such as Retail and Industrial.
